Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a minaret that forms part of the Qutub complex, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi.

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, or the Palace of Winds, is a palace in Jaipur, known for its unique architecture and numerous windows.

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is an arch-monument built during the 20th century in Mumbai, overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Ajanta Caves

The Ajanta Caves are rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments located in the Ajanta village of Maharashtra.
